Resumes Writing Tips

Resumes are the first direct link between an employee and a potential employee

Employers would only allocate 4.5 mins before he or she makes a verdict

Formate your resume Make it easy to understand,use wide margins,logical formate,utilized bullets, bold and italic fonts
Include the accomplishments You must always include how you can beneficial to the company
Make your resume suitable for the industry For an engineering or academic related position,make things simple and direct to the point
Career summary instead of objectives You can replace your 'Objectives' section with a 'Career Summary' so that you can give an overview of who you truly are
Use youe network Stay connected with a lot of people especially those who are related to the insudtry you wish to enter
